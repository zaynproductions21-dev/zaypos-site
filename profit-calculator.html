About this calculator

How is the figure calculated?
We multiply your covers × average ticket × an industry leak rate that scales with cash-vs-till variance frequency. The leak rate sits between 0.4% (never any variance — best in class) and 4.5% (most days — typical of unreconciled environments). It's a conservative estimate based on UK hospitality benchmarks.
