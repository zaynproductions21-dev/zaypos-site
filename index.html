📄 Invoice Processing

Scan an invoice. Stock updates itself.

Take a photo of a supplier invoice — even a crumpled handwritten one — upload it to ZayPOS, and the AI reads every item, quantity, and price. Review the extracted data, confirm, and your stock levels update automatically. No typing required.

  • ✓ Works with photos, PDFs, and scanned documents
  • ✓ Handles handwritten and poorly formatted invoices
  • ✓ Full audit trail — every invoice stored with original image
  • ✓ Batch processing — multiple invoices at once
How it works
Photo of invoice → AI extracts items & quantities → You confirm → Stock updates → Invoice archived.
