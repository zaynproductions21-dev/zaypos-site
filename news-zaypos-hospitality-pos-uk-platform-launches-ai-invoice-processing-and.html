ZayPOS Hospitality POS UK Platform Launches AI Invoice Processing and Automatic Payroll

New features in restaurant management software help UK food venues cut admin time with smarter till technology

31 May 2026 · ZayPOS

31 May 2026, UK —

ZayPOS, a leading hospitality POS UK platform, has launched AI invoice processing and automatic payroll features to help independent cafes, restaurants and bakeries reduce back-office administration. The new capabilities are integrated into ZayPOS's restaurant management software, which serves UK food and drink businesses with its smarter till system.

The AI invoice processing feature allows venue operators to capture supplier invoices simply by photographing them or uploading PDFs, automatically extracting key data and updating stock levels in real-time. Meanwhile, the automatic payroll system generates pay calculations directly from staff clock-in data linked to the platform's drag-and-drop rota planner, eliminating manual timesheet processing.

"These features address two of the biggest administrative pain points for independent food businesses," said a ZayPOS spokesperson. "By automating invoice processing and payroll calculations, venue owners can focus on what matters most - serving great food and creating exceptional customer experiences rather than spending hours on paperwork."

The new features complement ZayPOS's existing 50+ feature suite, including touch-optimised POS functionality across tablets, phones and desktops, Kitchen Display Systems with real-time order management, and QR table ordering that requires no customer app. The platform works with major UK payment providers including Stripe, Clover, Dojo, SumUp and Square, and includes comprehensive food safety compliance tools that are Food Standards Agency ready.

ZayPOS offers a 14-day free trial with no card required, migrates existing menus, staff and stock data within 24 hours, and provides a 60-day guarantee to save venues 5 hours per week or offers a full refund. The cloud-based platform is built specifically for busy service environments and maintains all data within GDPR-compliant UK infrastructure.
