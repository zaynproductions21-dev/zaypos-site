ZayPOS Launches Food Safety POS System with Built-in EHO Compliance for UK Venues

New hospitality POS UK platform automates temperature logs and delivers one-click compliance reports

31 May 2026 · ZayPOS

31 May 2026, UK —

ZayPOS has launched its integrated food safety POS solution designed specifically for UK cafes, restaurants and bakeries, combining point-of-sale functionality with automated EHO compliance reporting. The cloud-based hospitality POS UK system features digital temperature logging, automated compliance checklists, and one-click reporting capabilities that meet Food Standards Agency requirements.

The platform addresses a critical operational challenge for UK food venues by embedding food safety compliance directly into daily till operations. Digital fridge and freezer temperature logs replace manual paper systems, whilst automated opening, cleaning and closing checklists ensure consistent food safety protocols. The system sends SMS reminders to on-shift staff and generates comprehensive EHO-ready compliance reports at the click of a button.

"UK food venues are juggling complex compliance requirements alongside the daily pressures of service," said a ZayPOS spokesperson. "By integrating food safety monitoring directly into the POS system, venues can maintain rigorous standards without the administrative burden of separate systems and manual paperwork."

The food safety features form part of ZayPOS's all-in-one platform, which includes over 50 features spanning point-of-sale, kitchen display systems, stock management, staff scheduling and customer loyalty. The system offers a 14-day free trial without requiring card details and includes a 60-day guarantee promising to save venues five hours per week or provide a full refund. ZayPOS handles complete migration of menus, staff data and stock information, enabling venues to go live within 24 hours.

The platform supports multiple payment providers including Stripe, Clover, Dojo, SumUp and Square, and operates across tablets, phones and desktop computers without requiring specialist hardware. Additional features include QR table ordering, AI-powered invoice processing, automated payroll integration and staff food safety e-learning modules, all designed to streamline operations for independent UK hospitality businesses.
