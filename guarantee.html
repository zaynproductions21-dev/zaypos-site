A worked example of the 5 hours

Here's how the maths tends to land for a typical independent café — using the same indicative figures shown above.

Take a five-person café open six days a week. Before ZayPOS, the owner spends around three hours every Sunday building next week's rota in a spreadsheet and chasing shift swaps over WhatsApp. Supplier invoices — a dozen or so a week — get keyed into a stock sheet by hand, costing roughly two hours. The EHO and SFBB logbook, allergen matrix and temperature records eat another hour and a half across the week, and payroll and VAT reconciliation add about an hour on top. That's roughly seven and a half hours of pure admin, every single week.

With ZayPOS, the rota is generated from sales patterns in minutes, supplier invoices update stock from a photo, the logbook and allergen matrix live in one place, and payroll and VAT summaries are produced automatically. Even if only the rota and invoice savings fully land for you — the two biggest items — that's five hours back in your week before the compliance and payroll time is counted. The guarantee only asks you to reach five of those hours. If, after genuinely using the features, you don't, you email us and we refund every penny.

Common questions

If something here isn't covered, just email us — we'll add it.

Why are you offering this?

Because we're confident the AI rota, invoice and compliance features save you time — and because telling you "we're the best" is cheap. Putting our money on the line means you can switch without doing 30 hours of vendor due diligence first.

Does the 14-day free trial still apply?

Yes — the free trial runs first. No card needed for 14 days. The 60-day guarantee starts on the day you convert to a paid plan, so you effectively get 74 days of risk-free use.

Annual plans — do I get the full year refunded?

If you claim within 60 days of your first annual payment, yes — full annual amount refunded. After day 60, the standard refund policy applies (14-day window from purchase).

What counts as "giving the AI features a fair try"?

Generate at least one rota in the rota tool, scan at least one supplier invoice, and log at least one temperature check or opening checklist in the EHO logbook. We can see these in your account — takes about 20 minutes total to satisfy.

Can I keep using my data after the refund?

Of course — you can export your menu, stock, customer list and sales history at any time, even after refund. Your data is yours.

Is this the same as the refund policy?

No. The refund policy is our standard 14-day cooling-off window required by UK consumer law. The 60-day guarantee is a stronger commercial promise on top of that — tied to a specific outcome (5 hours/week saved).

What exactly does the guarantee refund — and what doesn't it cover?

It refunds your ZayPOS subscription fee in full for the period in question. It doesn't cover third-party charges such as your payment provider's transaction fees or any hardware you bought separately, since those aren't ours to refund. There's one claim per business, and it applies only once you're on a paid plan — not during the free trial.

How quickly will I get my money back?

We confirm your claim within 48 hours, with no retention call or interrogation, and the refund reaches your original payment method within 5–10 working days. The whole process on your side is a single email with a one-line reason — under five minutes.
