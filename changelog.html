15 January 2025 v2.4.0 New features
AI Meal Deal Builder & iOS Live Widget
Two major additions this month — an AI-powered meal deal suggestion engine and a live sales widget for your iPhone home screen.
  • AI meal deal suggestions based on your best-selling product combinations
  • iOS home screen widget showing live revenue, order count, and trending items
  • Bundle analytics dashboard — track meal deal revenue and attach rates
  • KDS order cards now show elapsed time with colour-coded urgency indicators
  • Fixed split payment rounding issue on orders with VAT
  • Staff PIN login now works correctly after screen lock on iPad
3 December 2024 v2.3.0 New features
Food Safety Module & EHO Inspector Pack
A complete food safety system is now available on the Complete plan — from digital temperature logs to a one-click EHO compliance report.
  • Digital temperature logging for fridges, freezers, and hot holding equipment
  • Customisable daily checklists (opening, cleaning, closing)
  • SMS reminders to on-shift staff for overdue checklists
  • One-click EHO-ready compliance report with date range filtering
  • Staff food safety e-learning modules with quiz assessments
  • Delivery logging now supports corrective action documentation
11 November 2024 v2.2.0 Improvements
Payroll Overhaul & Smart Rota Generation
Staff management gets a major upgrade — automatic payroll calculations and an AI-assisted rota generator that considers performance and fairness.
  • Automatic payroll calculation from clock-in/out data
  • Smart rota auto-generation — considers availability, performance, and fairness
  • Monthly payroll email reports sent to configurable recipients
  • Clock-in now validates against rota shifts automatically
  • Overtime alerts now configurable per staff member
  • Fixed shift swap notifications not sending in some time zones
7 October 2024 v2.1.0 New features
QR Table Ordering & Customer Loyalty
Customers can now order directly from their phones via QR codes — no app required. Plus a full loyalty programme with points tracking.
  • Unique QR codes generated per table — customers order from their phone
  • Orders from QR tables appear instantly on the KDS and POS
  • Points-based loyalty programme with customer profiles
  • Automated SMS and email review requests for takeaway orders
  • Table management dashboard with live occupancy status
2 September 2024 v2.0.0 Major release
ZayPOS 2.0 — Complete Platform Rebuild
A ground-up rebuild of the ZayPOS platform with a redesigned interface, real-time sync across all devices, and a new API for external integrations.
  • Completely redesigned UI — faster, cleaner, built for touch
  • Real-time data sync across all devices in your location
  • Multi-organisation support — manage multiple businesses from one account
  • Kitchen Display System (KDS) — live order view for kitchen staff
  • AI invoice processing — upload a photo, stock updates automatically
  • Secure API endpoint for external integrations
