Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025
Contents

In short: We use essential cookies to keep ZayPOS running, and optional analytics cookies to improve the product. You can manage your preferences at any time.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, and to provide information to website owners.

We use cookies on our marketing website (zaypos.co.uk) and within the ZayPOS application itself. Some cookies are essential for the platform to function; others help us understand how people use our product so we can improve it.

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for ZayPOS to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or setting preferences. You can set your browser to block these cookies, but parts of the Service will not work.

Analytics cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website and application. This helps us improve the product. Analytics cookies are only set with your consent. The data collected is aggregated and anonymised.

Functional cookies

Functional cookies enable additional features such as remembering your preferences and supporting payment processing. Stripe sets cookies during checkout to help prevent fraud and ensure payment security.

3. Third-party cookies

Some of our cookies are set by third-party services. These include:

  • Google Analytics — website usage statistics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment security and fraud prevention. Stripe Privacy Policy

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies for retargeting purposes.

4. Your choices

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie banner where you can accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking "Cookie settings" in the footer of our website.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete them. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using ZayPOS.

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5. Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.
