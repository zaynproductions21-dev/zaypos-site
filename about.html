Our story

Built by people who
know food businesses

ZayPOS was created because every other POS felt like it was designed by people who'd never worked a Saturday morning rush. We set out to build something different — something your team would actually enjoy using.

How it started

We were frustrated by
the same things you are

ZayPOS started with a simple problem: the POS systems available to small food businesses were either too basic, too expensive, or so complicated that staff needed days of training just to take an order.

We saw cafes drowning in paper temperature logs. We watched restaurant managers spending their Sundays manually calculating wages. We saw bakery owners losing stock because their system couldn't keep up.

So we built ZayPOS — a system that handles the hard stuff automatically, so you can focus on what you actually love: making great food and creating great experiences for your customers.

Today, ZayPOS is used by independent cafes, family-run restaurants, bakeries, and food trucks across the UK. Every feature exists because a real business owner told us they needed it.
