Step by step

You'll be taking orders
in under 30 minutes

Follow these six steps in order. Most businesses complete setup in one sitting — with time to make a coffee.

✓
~2 min
Create your account
Complete
Sign up with your email address to start your 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link to activate.
  • ✓ Enter your name and email
  • ✓ Choose a secure password
  • ✓ Confirm your email address
  • ✓ Select your plan (you can change this anytime during the trial)
✓
~5 min
Set up your business
Complete
Go to Settings → Organisation and fill in your business details. These appear on customer receipts and compliance reports.
  • ✓ Business name, address, and phone number
  • ✓ VAT registration number (if applicable)
  • ✓ Receipt footer text (e.g. "Thank you for visiting!")
  • ✓ Your business logo (appears on receipts and reports)
3
~10 min
Build your product catalogue
You are here
Add your menu items with prices and categories. Products appear as tappable buttons on the order screen — so good organisation here makes your staff's job easier.
  • → Go to Products → Catalogue → Add Product
  • → Create categories first (e.g. Hot Drinks, Food, Cakes)
  • → Add each item with name, price, and category
  • → Set up modifiers for items with options (e.g. milk type, size)
View product guide → Bulk import via Excel
💡
Tip: Got more than 20 products? Use our Excel import template to upload your whole menu in one go — download the template, fill it in, and upload under Products → Import.
Watch: Adding products (3 min)
4
~5 min
Add your staff
Up next
Create accounts for everyone who'll use ZayPOS. Each person gets their own 4-digit PIN for fast, secure login at the till.
  • → Go to Staff → Accounts → Add Staff
  • → Set each person's name, role (Staff / Manager / Admin), and PIN
  • → Staff role: can take orders only
  • → Manager role: can view reports and manage stock
  • → Admin role: full access to all settings
Staff management guide
5
~5 min
Connect your payment terminal
Almost there
Link your card payment terminal so ZayPOS can send charges directly to it. ZayPOS supports Clover, SumUp, Dojo, and Square.
  • → Go to Settings → Payments
  • → Select your payment provider and enter your API credentials
  • → Run a test transaction to confirm the connection
  • → Configure cash drawer settings if applicable
View payment integrations
💳
Don't have a card terminal yet? You can still use ZayPOS with cash-only mode. Add your terminal later under Settings → Payments.
6
~2 min
Take your first order 🎉
Final step
You're ready. Open ZayPOS on your tablet or phone, log in with your staff PIN, select your order type, and start taking orders.
  • → Open app.zaypos.co.uk on your device
  • → Enter your 4-digit staff PIN
  • → Select order type: Dine-in, Takeaway, or Delivery
  • → Tap products to add them to the cart
  • → Hit Charge and process the payment

Common questions

Getting started FAQ

How long does setup take? ⌄
Most businesses complete the core setup (products, staff, payments) in 30–60 minutes. If you have a large menu, use our Excel bulk import to speed things up significantly.
Can I change my plan during the trial? ⌄
Yes, you can switch between Starter, Pro, and Complete at any point during your 14-day trial. Everything is fully accessible on all plans during the trial period.
Do I need to buy any hardware? ⌄
No. ZayPOS works on any tablet, phone, or desktop you already own. A receipt printer is optional — we support network ESC/POS printers if you need one.
What if I need help during setup? ⌄
Every trial user gets access to our onboarding team. You can book a free 30-minute call, email us at [email protected], or chat with us during business hours.
Can I import my existing product list? ⌄
Yes. Download our Excel template, fill in your products with names, prices, and categories, then upload it under Products → Import. Your whole catalogue will be added instantly.
How do my staff log in? ⌄
Staff use a personal 4-digit PIN to log into ZayPOS — fast, secure, and no passwords to remember. You set the PINs for each staff member from the Staff → Accounts area.
