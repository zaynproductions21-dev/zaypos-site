ZayPOS Hospitality POS UK System Preps Venues for Summer 2026 Trading Rush

Smarter Till Technology and Cafe POS System Features Help Independent Operators Handle Peak Season

31 May 2026 · ZayPOS

31 May 2026, UK —

ZayPOS, a leading hospitality POS UK provider, is helping independent cafes and restaurants prepare for the anticipated summer 2026 trading surge with its comprehensive smarter till solution featuring touch-fast ordering and real-time stock management. The all-in-one cloud POS system offers over 50 features designed specifically for UK food and drink establishments, with a 14-day free trial requiring no payment card details.

The cafe POS system addresses critical summer trading challenges through its touch-optimised interface that works across tablets, phones and desktops without requiring specialist hardware. Key features include real-time out-of-stock detection to prevent disappointing customers, split payment options supporting cash, card and bank transfer methods, and integration with major UK payment providers including Stripe, Clover, Dojo, SumUp and Square.

"Summer trading presents unique challenges for independent hospitality businesses, from managing increased customer volumes to maintaining stock levels during peak demand periods," said a ZayPOS spokesperson. "Our platform combines rapid order processing with intelligent inventory management, helping venues maximise revenue opportunities whilst maintaining service quality during their busiest months."

The system includes advanced operational features particularly valuable during high-volume periods, such as a real-time Kitchen Display System with per-station organisation and ticket-time analytics, QR table ordering requiring no customer app downloads, and AI-powered invoice processing from photos or PDFs. Staff management tools include secure PIN logins, integrated clock-in/out functionality linked to rotas, and automated payroll generation from time data.

ZayPOS offers comprehensive onboarding support, migrating existing menus, staff data and stock information within 24 hours, backed by a 60-day guarantee promising to save operators five hours weekly or provide a full refund. The GDPR-compliant system also includes Food Standards Agency-ready compliance features with digital temperature logging, automated checklists and one-click EHO reporting.
